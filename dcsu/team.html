Haley Greene

Vice President & Secretary

Haley is an Advertising and Communications and Rhetorical studies major. She was born and raised in Los Angeles California. Haley is known for her exceptional communication skills and ability to connect with diverse audiences. Her passion for effective dialogue and creating a platform for inclusive discussions propelled the idea and establishment of DCSU. Haley's focus on promoting diversity of thought and ensuring that everyone's voice was heard became integral to the team's foundation.
