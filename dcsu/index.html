Member

As a member, you will be part of our community and allowed to come to all events, meetings, and guest speaker lectures.

02

Member Responsibilities & Perks

Must attend a weekly meeting to remain an active member.

As a Member, you will be part of our community and gain access to all events, meetings, and guest speaker lectures.

Once you are a General Member you can talk to our Exec board about becoming a part of the debate competition team. Competing members will be chosen on performance and skill and debate practice.

As a Debate competitor, you will go to training and practice sessions.

Being a Team Member means you will be a part of the competition team and allowed to compete in intercollegiate competitions.

If you are an experienced debater, you may reach out to join the competition team now.

03

Process to Become a Member

Fill out an application form

Attend an info session

Fill out attendance form

Wait to hear back from us!

04

EXEC Board

If you have an interest in being a part of the DSCU team and contributing in a greater way, our Exec board is the place for you. You can hold a position on Exec and not be a part of the debate team if you choose.

You will be able to apply for EXEC once you are already a member.

If you are interested in EXEC please reach out to hrgreene@syr.edu

All open positions are on the EXEC page
