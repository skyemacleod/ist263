THE NEED

Why Mirror

Eating disorders affect 30 million Americans, yet up to 80% never receive care due to stigma, cost, or lack of access. Traditional treatment can cost as much as $250,000, leaving most people without help. Mirror exists to change this reality.

We are building the first free, peer-led, clinically informed digital platform designed for eating disorder recovery. With Mirror, no one has to choose between silence and debt recovery is accessible, safe, and identity-inclusive.
